The project

The birth and death of a star, create layers and layers of things that eventually forms a star and then destroy it. Using smoke and lasers like Rufus du sol used. Create a plastic box that you fill with smoke and small lasers projects the visuals with sounds in it. Can create a structure inside it. We use shapes that are randomly generated of whatever shape and sizes that fit together to form the star.. showing that whatever the shape size each and every effort counts

Depicting the layers of real life stars and heroes whose work is based on hundreds of people. It's not a lone effort but a collaboration and the fact that if you remove these collaborations, the star dies.

We can create sounds and visuals in touch designer pd Ophelia openframework.etc

The idea Sparked when we talked about the great king Maharana Pratap Singh who kept the Indian spirit alive and gave Indians reason to fight against the Mughal invaders. Many people helped him and he helped them in return. Names of them are forgotten but they still live within and through the tales of the king and his kingdom and forts
